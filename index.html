Sample Description:

The CT DEEP Monitoring and Assessment Program took water samples at the boat ramp and what locals refer to as the “swim area” on 6/14/2019. The boat ramp had a mix of pollen as well as a pretty good cyanbacteria bloom. The samples were almost exclusively Anabaena affinis, a cyanobacteria. This genus is known to produce the toxins Anatoxin a, Microcystin, and Cylindrospermopsin.
